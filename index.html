Explore Coimbatore

EVENTS

Valparai Hill Station

Valparai is a peaceful hill station known for its breathtaking views. Visitors can enjoy trekking, wildlife spotting in the nearby Anamalai Tiger Reserve

Adiyogi Statue

Adiyogi Statue, dedicated to Lord Shiva. This site offers spiritual experiences, meditation sessions, and a serene environment for visitors.

Siruvani Waterfalls

Visitors can enjoy scenic hikes, relaxing near the waterfalls, and experiencing the natural beauty of this region, which is also known for its sweet water.

Gedee car Museum

The museum showcases a variety of his inventions and offers a glimpse into the technological progress made in the region.

FESTIVALS

Aravan Festival

Known as "Koothandavar Thiruvizha" is festival celebrated in Coimbatore city since ancient times.It is celebrated for the Aravan, a major character in Hindu epic Mahabarata.

Koniamman Car Festival

Koniamman annual care festival is grand festival celebrated in Coimbatore in the month of Tamil month Masi. During this festivals thousands and thousands of people engage in temple car dragging.

Panguni Uthiram Festival

It celebrated by farmers along the banks of Noyyal River.It is conducted in month of Aani.The ritual forms part of Indra festival.I is called "Perur Puranam" by Kachiappa Munivar

Lord Jagannath Festival

The International Society for Krishna Consciousness (ISKCON) organizes the Jagannath Rath Yatra festival to celebrate Lord Jagannath, his sister Subhadra, and his brother Balabhadra.
